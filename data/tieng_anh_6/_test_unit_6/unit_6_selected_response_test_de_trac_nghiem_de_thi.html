ROAD TRIP GUIDE TO THE SAHARA OF MOROCCO

Description

The Sahara is one of the world’s most spectacular destinations. With the full of arid sand and hot climate, the Sahara is not suitable for all travellers. Tourists can still take a road trip to the Sahara Desert of Morocco, which is located in the south east of the country. 

Tour guide 

Here are some useful tips for tourists to follow:

  • Plan when to visit: The best time to visit is from October to April. Travelling between February and March is a bad choice because of dangerous sand storms. 
  • You must bring plenty of food and take extra water to keep yourself hydrated. 
  • Don’t camp near the roads to avoid some crazy drivers. Also, you mustn’t drive at night. 
  • Dress wisely: Wearing light-coloured clothes will help you not to absorb too much heat. And covering your head and your face by a hat and a scarf is good for avoiding heatstroke. 
  • Bring a compass, a map or a guide book with you. 
  • Don’t get too close to some certain plants, especially those looking attractive because most of them have poison.
