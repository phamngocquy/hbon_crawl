HA GIANG’S KHAU VAI LOVE MARKET FESTIVAL

 

Holding The Khau Vai Love Market is unique to locals in Meo Vac district of Ha Giang’s mountainous province.  Unlike others, Khau Vai Love Market is interesting because people here do not sell or buy any goods, but to meet ex-lovers.

According to folklore, a man and a woman were in love but were forced to break up because they were from different ethnic groups. A serious fight happened between the two tribes, so the couple decided to part ways in order to stop it. However, they promised to meet once every year in the Khau Vai market on the 27th of the third lunar month.

On this day, local ethnic women and men put on their best traditional costumes and go to the market. There, they are free to meet their former lovers in public. Husbands and wives go to the market together and start looking for their past lovers among the crowd. They do not get angry or sad because the meeting is just a moment and does not affect their daily life.

The festival remains till today for its meaning, and this northern love market has become a symbol of love and romance.

 

Adapted from https://www.vir.com.vn/ha-giangs-khau-vai-love-market-festival-to-start-in-april-28-67392.html
