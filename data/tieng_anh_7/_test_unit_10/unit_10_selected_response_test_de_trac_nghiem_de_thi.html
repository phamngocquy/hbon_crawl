Thông tin

Information text

Read the text and choose the correct option(s) for each question.

Fossil fuels are non-renewable energy sources. They include oil, coal, and natural gas. They are used throughout the world to power everything from cars to lights. Statistically, fossil fuels are some of the cheapest sources of fuel on the planet. However, people cannot deny their disadvantages. Firstly, they can cause environmental pollution. This is because they give off carbon dioxide when they are burned. Secondly, they can run out soon. It takes very long time and specific conditions to form a fossil fuel. When we use it up, it is hard for us to find another source immediately. Thirdly, taking them from underground can be dangerous. Workers can face serious health problems when doing this task. Nowadays, we are finding more and more other sources to replace fossil fuels. Renewable sources including solar, wind and hydro power are thought to be cleaner and safer.
