IV – READING

Read the following text and choose the answers for the following questions.

Here are the four tips that help you to stay in shape. 

1. Eat a Variety of Foods

Eating different foods will help you to get various vitamins that your body needs. It is also important that you have at least five servings of fruits and vegetables a day.

2. Drink Water & Milk

Kids need calcium to build strong bones, and milk is a great source of this mineral. If you are 4 to 8 years old, drink two cups of milk a day. If you're 9 or older, aim for three cups of milk per day. Avoid sugary drinks like sodas and juice cocktails because they contain a lot of sugar. Sugar just adds calories, not vitamins.

3. Limit Screen Time

Screen time is the amount of time you spend watching TV, playing video games, and using a smartphone or computer. Too much screen time can make you become short-sighted and even obese. You should only spend no more than 2 hours a day on screen time, not counting computer use at school. 

4. Be Active

Instead of watching TV or playing computer games, it is healthier for you to play some sports, do regular exercises, or go out with friends.
